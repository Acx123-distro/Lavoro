🌍 1. CORE IDEA (ORIGINAL THINKING)

Lavoro is a Ghana-focused digital ecosystem designed to solve one major problem:

People in Ghana struggle to find trusted work, hire skilled people, and sell services/products safely in one place.

The platform combines:

Freelancing (Upwork style)
Services marketplace (Fiverr style but simpler)
Product buying/selling (Jumia-style logic)
Foreign job link aggregation (paid access layer)
🎯 2. CORE PROBLEM WE ARE SOLVING

In Ghana and similar markets:

Hiring is informal (WhatsApp, Facebook, word-of-mouth)
No structured trust system
Freelancers cannot access global jobs easily
Businesses cannot reliably hire verified workers
No unified system for jobs + services + products
